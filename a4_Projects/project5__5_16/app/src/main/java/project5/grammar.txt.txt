<s> ::= <np> <vp>
<np> ::= <dp> <adjp> <n> | <pn>
<dp> ::= every | some
<adjp> ::= <adj> | <adj> <adjp>
<adj> ::= old | young | tall | short | clever | lazy | brave
<n> ::= wizard | dragon | knight | castle | village | forest | river
<pn> ::= Merlin | Arthur | Morgana | Lancelot | Guinevere
<vp> ::= <tv> <np> | <iv>
<tv> ::= protects | seeks | finds | builds | destroys
<iv> ::= rests | dreams | sleeps | awakens
